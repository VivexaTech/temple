Our History

The Legacy of Devotion

For centuries, this sacred ground has been a beacon of hope and spirituality. Shri Ram Mandir stands as a testament to unwavering faith, architectural brilliance, and the eternal teachings of the Ramayana.

Our Mission: To foster a community of love, righteousness (Dharma), and peace, welcoming devotees from all walks of life to experience the divine presence of Maryada Purushottam Ram.
