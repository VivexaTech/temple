About The Mandir

A symbol of eternal faith, cultural heritage, and spiritual awakening.

The Legacy

A Journey of Devotion and Resilience

Shri Ram Janmabhoomi holds profound spiritual significance for millions across the globe as the birthplace of Maryada Purushottam Prabhu Shri Ram. For centuries, this sacred land has witnessed the unwavering devotion, patience, and resolve of saints and devotees.

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by the eternal teachings of the Ramayana, we strive to serve humanity and foster a community rooted in Dharma.

Dharma (Righteousness)

Upholding the moral and ethical values demonstrated by Lord Ram to create a harmonious and just society.

Seva (Selfless Service)

Providing regular Annadaan (food distribution), medical camps, and educational support to the underprivileged.

Bhakti (Devotion)

Facilitating daily pujas, aartis, and grand spiritual discourses to help devotees connect with the divine.

Architectural Marvel

The temple is a masterpiece of the traditional **Nagara style** of temple architecture. Built to last for over a millennium, it stands as a testament to ancient Indian engineering and craftsmanship.

380 Ft

Length (East-West), featuring 392 intricately carved pillars.

161 Ft

Total height of the majestic main Shikhar (spire).

No Iron

Constructed entirely of stone with locking mechanisms, ensuring zero iron rust.

5 Mandapas

Including Nritya Mandap, Rang Mandap, and Prarthana Mandap.

Shri Ram Mandir
